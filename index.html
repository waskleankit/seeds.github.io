About Us

Our Story

Gromantra Seeds was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to provide gardeners with the highest quality, non-GMO seeds that produce exceptional results. What started as a small family business has grown into a trusted source for gardeners across the country.

We carefully select and test all our seeds to ensure they meet our strict standards for germination rate, purity, and performance. Our team includes experienced horticulturists who are passionate about helping our customers succeed in their gardening endeavors.

Our Values

At Gromantra Seeds, we believe in sustainable gardening practices, preserving heirloom varieties, and promoting biodiversity. We're committed to offering seeds that are never genetically modified and are always open-pollinated when possible.

We take pride in our exceptional customer service and are always available to answer your gardening questions. Your success is our success!
