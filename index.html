About Us

Our Story

Elite Seeds was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to provide gardeners with the highest quality, non-GMO seeds that produce exceptional results. What started as a small family business has grown into a trusted source for gardeners across the country.

We carefully select and test all our seeds to ensure they meet our strict standards for germination rate, purity, and performance. Our team includes experienced horticulturists who are passionate about helping our customers succeed in their gardening endeavors.

Our Values

At Elite Seeds, we believe in sustainable gardening practices, preserving heirloom varieties, and promoting biodiversity. We're committed to offering seeds that are never genetically modified and are always open-pollinated when possible.

We take pride in our exceptional customer service and are always available to answer your gardening questions. Your success is our success!

Hosting Information

Free Hosting Options for Your Website

There are several excellent free hosting options available for static websites like this one. Here are the best choices:

GitHub Pages

Free hosting directly from your GitHub repository. Perfect for static sites with custom domain support.

How to set up:

  1. Create a GitHub account
  2. Create a new repository named username.github.io
  3. Upload your website files
  4. Enable GitHub Pages in repository settings

Netlify

Free tier with continuous deployment from Git, form handling, and HTTPS.

How to set up:

  1. Create a Netlify account
  2. Drag and drop your website folder
  3. Or connect your GitHub repository for auto-deploy
  4. Configure your custom domain

Vercel

Excellent platform for static sites with global CDN and automatic SSL.

How to set up:

  1. Create a Vercel account
  2. Import your project from GitHub/GitLab
  3. Deploy with zero configuration
  4. Add your custom domain

Connecting a Custom Domain

To use your own domain name (like GromantraSeeds.in):

  1. Purchase a domain from a registrar (Namecheap, GoDaddy, etc.)
  2. In your hosting platform's settings, add your custom domain
  3. Update your domain's DNS settings:
    • For GitHub Pages: Create A records pointing to 185.199.108.153
    • For Netlify/Vercel: Use CNAME record pointing to your site's URL
  4. Wait for DNS propagation (up to 48 hours)

Admin Panel for Static Sites

Since we're not using a database, you can manage content through:

  • JavaScript-based admin panel (like the one included in this demo)
  • Git-based CMS (update content directly in GitHub)
  • Headless CMS options like Netlify CMS or Forestry
  • Local storage for small amounts of data
